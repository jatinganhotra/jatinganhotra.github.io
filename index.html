I am a Research Software Engineer at IBM Research AI, Thomas J. Watson Research Center. I graduated from University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign in Fall'15, with Masters in Computer Science.
My research interests are in Deep Learning approaches, with a primary focus on Dialog systems.
